Conclusions
  • Sudden cardiac deaths kill millions of people around the world each year.
  • Most common causes of SCD is coronary artery disease.
  • Prevention strategy of SCD include medications, ICD, and early CPR.
  • Exercise is good for your heart. Exercise related SCA is rare esp. if you exercises regularly.
